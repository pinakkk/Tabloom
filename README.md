## Tabloom

Tabloom is an open-source Chrome extension designed to enhance the personal browsing experience, making it more productive and smooth for those who value focus and organization. By aligning your new tab page with your mood, tracking productive time, displaying motivational quotes, and offering an integrated to-do list, Tabloom helps you create a workspace that supports your productivity and keeps you inspired.

### Note: Tabloom is currently under development, and new features are being added continuously.

## Features Under Development 🌟
1. Mood-Based Themes: Pick a mood (calm, focused, creative, etc.), and Tabloom will match it with a suitable theme, from colors to background music.
2. Dynamic Backgrounds: Automatic wallpaper or animation changes aligned with your mood.
3. Inspirational Quotes: Uplifting quotes for motivation with each new tab.
4. Productivity Tracker: Monitors total productive time to help you stay focused.
5. Built-in To-Do List: Quickly add, organize, and track tasks directly in your new tab.
6. Shortcut & Bookmark Customization: Easily add shortcuts and pinned bookmarks for an organized browsing experience.
7. Seamless Integration: Personalization and productivity tools appear every time you open a new tab.

## Mood Options 🎨
Tabloom’s mood options help you create the ideal workspace:

- Calm: Gentle colors and tranquil visuals to keep you relaxed.
- Energetic: Bold, bright themes to give you a boost.
- Focused: Minimalistic designs to remove distractions.
- Creative: Vibrant and inspiring visuals for brainstorming.
- Relaxed: Soothing animations for a mellow browsing experience.

## Productivity & Organization 🌱
- Productivity Tracking: Activate productivity mode to track time spent on tasks, giving you insights into your work habits and helping you meet goals.

- Inspirational Quotes: Motivational quotes appear on each tab, perfect for quick encouragement to stay on track.

- Integrated To-Do List: Add tasks, check them off as you complete them, and keep track of your priorities without leaving the tab. Perfect for managing daily goals, ideas, or reminders.

## Installation
1. Clone this repository:
```
git clone https://github.com/yourusername/Tabloom.git
```
2. Open Chrome and go to chrome://extensions/.
3. Enable "Developer mode" (toggle in the top right).
4. Click "Load unpacked" and select the Tabloom folder.
5. Start experiencing Tabloom’s fully personalized and organized new tab!

## Usage 📖
1. Open the Tabloom extension to select your mood.
2. Enable productivity tracking and check your stats as needed.
3. Use the to-do list to manage your tasks directly from the new tab.
4. Add bookmarks and shortcuts for easy access.
5. Enjoy personalized themes, motivational quotes, and productivity insights every time you open a new tab.
